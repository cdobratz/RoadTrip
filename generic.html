We are a Full House

10.05.2024

Welcome to our vibrant household at Sunshine Acres Children's Home! We're a unique family of seven energetic boys and one spirited girl, guided by a devoted husband and wife team who pour their hearts into creating a home filled with love, learning, and adventure.

Our family thrives on exploration and discovery. Whether we're hiking through national parks, engaging in competitive sports, or embarking on new adventures, we believe in creating memories that last a lifetime. These shared experiences build more than just fun moments – they foster teamwork, resilience, and unbreakable bonds between us all.

At the heart of our home lies a deep commitment to faith and personal growth. We're dedicated to teaching our children about Jesus while helping them develop into responsible, tech-savvy members of the modern world. Each day brings opportunities to learn, grow, and support one another as we navigate life's journey together.

In our AV family, every child is valued, understood, and encouraged to embrace their unique gifts. We're not just a group living under one roof – we're a united team, working together to build a foundation for a bright and promising future. Join us as we share our stories, adventures, and the daily joys of growing together as a family.
